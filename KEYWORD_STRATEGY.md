## ðŸ“ KEYWORD USAGE GUIDELINES

### On-Page Optimization

**For Each Page:**

1. **Title Tag:**
   - Include primary keyword
   - Keep under 60 characters
   - Add modifiers: 2026, Best, Guide, Packages
   - Example: "Bir Billing Paragliding Packages 2026 | Best Prices | Aerial Bir Travel"

2. **Meta Description:**
   - Include primary + 1-2 secondary keywords
   - Include pricing/offer
   - Include CTA
   - 150-160 characters
   - Example: "Book Bir Billing Paragliding at â‚¹2,500! Expert pilots, safety guaranteed. Dharamshala & Manali tours available. 10,000+ happy travelers. Call now!"

3. **H1 (Page Headline):**
   - Exact match or close variant of primary keyword
   - Example: "Bir Billing Paragliding & Tour Packages - Best Prices 2026"

4. **First 100 Words:**
   - Must include primary keyword
   - Natural, readable
   - Example: "Looking for Bir Billing paragliding packages? Aerial Bir Travel offers the best paragliding experiences in Bir Billing at competitive prices..."

5. **Keyword Density:**
   - Primary keyword: 3-5 times per 1000 words (0.3-0.5%)
   - Secondary keywords: 2-3 times
   - LSI keywords: Naturally throughout

6. **Image ALT Text:**
   - Include keyword
   - Descriptive
   - Example: "Professional tandem paragliding in Bir Billing Himachal Pradesh"
